Jovie's BINANGKAL RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ½ cups All-Purpose Flour
  • ¾ cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • ¼ tsp Baking Soda
  • 2 large Eggs
  • ¼ cup Evaporated Milk (or fresh milk)
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil

TOOLS

  • Mixing Bowls
  • Measuring Cups and Spoons
  • Wire Whisk or Spatula
  • Deep Frying Pan or Pot
  • Slotted Spoon or Spider Strainer
  • Tray or Plate with Paper Towels
  • Clean Hands or Plastic Gloves

The Procedures (Instructions)

  1. Mix the Dry Ingredients
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients
  3. Combine to Form Dough
  4. Shape the Balls
  5. Coat with Sesame Seeds
  6. Deep Frying (Crucial Step)
  7. Drain and Serve
